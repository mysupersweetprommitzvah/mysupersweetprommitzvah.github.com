Mission Statement

To create a repository of tools for throwing open source parties that are a combination of Sweet Sixteens, Proms, and Bar/Bat Mitzvahs.

History

My Super Sweet Prom Mitzvah was first discussed at a 2012 summer lunch eaten at White Horse Tavern in Allston, MA.
